8. Rescheduling / Late Arrivals / Cancellation

If the Client needs to reschedule a session, the deposit will be applied to the rescheduled session provided that notice is given at least 3 days in advance. Any rescheduling request made less than 3 days before the session will result in the forfeiture of the deposit.

Rescheduled sessions must occur within 6 months from the original date, and a session can only be rescheduled twice. For any third rescheduling, a new deposit will be required.

If the session is canceled, the deposit will be refunded only if the cancellation is made at least 5 days before the scheduled session.

In respect of the photographer's time, please note that if you are more than 30 - 45 minutes late, your session may be canceled, or you will be charged on a pro-rata basis of 350 QAR per hour on top of your session. In respect to last minute sessions that are being booked within less than the 3 days for rescheduling and 5 days for cancellation policy, any cancellation or rescheduling will result in forfeiture of the deposit.
